Norwegian Delights

Food

Norwegian food is not necisarilly the best in the world but there a couple that are absolutly amazing!! Some popular favorites are: Waffles, Pinnekjott, and fish.

Waffles

waffles in Norway are amazing and super easy to make. Here is even a recipe that you can use in creating your own!

Pinnekjott

Pinnekjott is a wonderful dish that directly translated means"Stick Meat" as in order to cook these lamb ribs you need to smoke it with small wooden rods and then add tons of salt to give it that savory flavor. There is so much salt that you will be drinking so much water that you will still be thirsty no matter how much your drink.

Fish

Norway has always been known for their fish. They are especially known for all their Salmon farms. These farms are top producers for places in Asia and Europe. These farms are all over Norway and would be uncommon to see a house in the middle of no where becuase of maitenance for the fish farms. These farms bring in millions of salmon every year which doubles for cheap salmon and caviar.
